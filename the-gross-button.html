Three flavors of wrong

Select your scene

Gross

Bright, tense, and alien. Adds unstable dissonance that cuts through the mix. Perfect for leads and upper-register riffs.

Disgust

Darker and more sinister. A heavier, uglier flavor of dissonance that adds weight and menace to rhythm parts.

Putrid

Maximum dissonance. Three-note chaos that sounds wrong in the best way — no mercy.

The two controls

Nuke & Sicken

Input gain

Nuke

Controls how hard your guitar DI hits the pitch engine. Turning up NUKE pushes the input signal harder before pitch shifting, increasing aggression, instability, and harmonic chaos.

Great for dialing in how violent the effect reacts to your playing, from subtle tension to full meltdown.

Wet signal mix

Sicken

Blends the effected signal with your dry tone. Lower settings keep things controlled and usable, while higher settings fully embrace the dissonance.

Perfect for automation, transitions, and finding the exact balance between clarity and absolute grossness.
