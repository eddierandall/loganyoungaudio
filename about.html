About Logan Young Audio

A producer building the tools he needed

Logan Young Audio started the way most useful tools do — with a problem nobody else was solving. Logan is a working producer and artist inside modern heavy music, and every plugin here began as something he built for his own sessions before it became something you can buy.

Built from the studio, not a spec sheet

There's no product roadmap dictating what comes next — just whatever Logan is fighting with in his own mixes. If a knob doesn't solve a real creative problem, it doesn't make it into the plugin.

Small, deliberate, and growing

The catalog is intentionally small right now. Each release takes the time it needs, and the goal is a handful of tools people actually reach for — not a library nobody opens twice.

What guides it
